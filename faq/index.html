Frequently Asked Questions

How do I get to the Unplugged retreat?

Here are the directions to the Unplugged retreat. You just need to take a left at the light then head down toward the ocean. Once you reach the ocean, convert you car into a boat and sail.

What kind of team is right for the Unplugged retreat?

It honestly doesn't matter! We accept all shapes and sizes and personalities. You can be a team of 3 or more. Sorry, 2 people on this retreat won't work. We recommend couple's counseing instead.

What size teams can you accommodate?

As mentioned before, we accept groups of 3 or more. Our maximum size group would have to be 50.

Is there really no wifi available?

As much as we don't want you to use wifi and be on technology, we do actually have a computer room for those times when you desperately need wifi.
